Hap Adobe Plugin for Apple Silicon

This package installs Hap import/playback and export plugins for Adobe Creative
Cloud on Apple Silicon Macs.

Exports are available through Adobe Media Encoder and Premiere Pro. Hap movie
import/playback is available in Premiere Pro and After Effects.

This Apple Silicon release builds on the original Hap Exporter for Adobe
Creative Cloud and the Codec Foundation Adobe CC framework.

Original principal contributors include:

-  Greg Bakker
-  Richard Sykes
-  Tom Butterworth
-  Nick Zinovenko
